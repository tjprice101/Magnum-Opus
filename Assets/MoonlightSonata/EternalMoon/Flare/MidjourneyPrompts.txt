================================================================================
  ETERNAL MOON — FLARE TEXTURES
  Midjourney Prompt Reference
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Lens flare and light bloom textures used at swing peaks, impact moments,
  and phase transition eclipse flashes. These are drawn by CustomParticles.GenericFlare()
  and the CrescentTipBloom system in EternalMoonVFX.cs. They render as additive-blend
  sprites on black backgrounds, so they must be white/colored light on pure black (#000000).

USAGE IN CODE:
  - GenericFlare() calls during CrescendoFinaleVFX (eclipse flare cascade)
  - OnHitImpact crit bursts (layered dark-to-light flash sequence)
  - PhaseTransitionEclipse (brief dark-to-light burst when combo advances)
  - GodRaySystem.CreateBurst for radial light burst at Crescendo
  - Drawn with {A=0} premultiplied alpha trick for additive glow stacking

DIMENSIONS: 128x128 or 256x256, pure black (#000000) background
COLOR PALETTE:
  - Night Void:     RGB(40, 10, 60)    — deepest shadow, outer aura
  - Deep Indigo:    RGB(75, 0, 130)    — eclipse darkness ring
  - Violet Body:    RGB(138, 43, 226)  — primary flare body
  - Ice Blue Flare: RGB(135, 206, 250) — bright flare spikes
  - Glacial Blue:  RGB(170, 225, 255) — icy inner crescent accent
  - Moon White: RGB(230, 235, 255) — cool flare center core

VISUAL IDENTITY: "Moonrise over dark waters" — asymmetric crescent-shaped light,
  tidal light ripples, not standard symmetric lens flares.

REFERENCE: SandboxLastPrism/Flare/Simple Lens Flare_11.png, flare_16.png
