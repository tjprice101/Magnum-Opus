================================================================================
  ETERNAL MOON — PIXEL PARTICLE TEXTURES
  Midjourney Prompt Reference
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Very small pixel-art scale particle sprites used by the custom dust system.
  These are drawn as individual dust motes with Dust.NewDustPerfect() and are
  tinted, scaled, and rotated per-frame by custom behavior classes. Each pixel
  particle represents a tiny elemental fragment: water droplets, moon shards,
  crescent slivers, or starpoint twinkles.

USAGE IN CODE:
  - TidalMoonDust — flowing water-like particles along the blade during swings,
    sinusoidal drift path, velocity decay, custom lifetime. Uses small droplet
    or water fragment shapes.
  - LunarMote — crescent sliver particles that orbit the blade tip with orbital
    mechanics. Needs tiny crescent moon shapes.
  - StarPointDust — tidal foam sparkle points with rotation and twinkle behavior.
    Small 4-pointed or 6-pointed star shapes.
  - ResonantPulseDust — expanding ring particles (handled by Orbs, not Pixel).
  - Contrast sparkles spawned by MoonlightVFXLibrary.SpawnContrastSparkle()
  - All drawn at small scale (0.15-0.5f multiplier) with dust.noGravity = true

DIMENSIONS: 8x8, 12x12, 16x16, or 32x32 maximum. Must have clean, defined
  edges for sharp rendering at small scale. Can be on black or transparent
  background depending on blend mode.

COLOR PALETTE (particles are tinted at runtime, so base should be white/neutral
  or pre-colored — provide both options):
  - Night Void:     RGB(40, 10, 60)    — dark trailing motes
  - Violet Body:    RGB(138, 43, 226)  — primary body motes
  - Ice Blue Flare: RGB(135, 206, 250) — bright highlight motes
  - Glacial Blue:  RGB(170, 225, 255) — icy accent motes
  - Moon White: RGB(230, 235, 255) — cool sparkle motes

REFERENCE: SandboxLastPrism/Pixel/Flare.png, PartiGlow.png
