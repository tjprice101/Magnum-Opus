================================================================================
  ETERNAL MOON — ORB TEXTURES
  Midjourney Prompt Reference
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Circular soft glow textures used for multi-layer bloom stacking, tidal wake
  glow points, crescent tip bloom layers, and halo ring effects. These are the
  most frequently drawn textures in the EternalMoon VFX pipeline — every frame
  of a swing draws 3-5 layered orb sprites per wake point and at the blade tip.

USAGE IN CODE:
  - MagnumTextureRegistry.GetSoftGlow() — the primary orb texture, drawn at
    multiple scales and colors for bloom layering (see DrawTidalWake which draws
    3 layers per wake point, and DrawCrescentTipBloom which draws 5 layers)
  - CustomParticles.HaloRing() — hollow ring expansion effect on impact
  - GlowRenderer.GlowLayer[] MoonlightGlowProfile — 4-layer glow stack
  - PreDrawInWorld 5-layer bloom on the dropped item
  - All drawn with {A=0} premultiplied alpha and additive blending

DIMENSIONS: Various sizes — 32x32 (small mote glow), 64x64 (standard bloom),
  128x128 (large halo/impact glow). Must be perfectly centered with smooth
  feathered falloff to fully transparent/black at edges.

COLOR PALETTE:
  - Night Void:     RGB(40, 10, 60)    — largest, softest outer orb layer
  - Deep Indigo:    RGB(75, 0, 130)    — second outer layer
  - Violet Body:    RGB(138, 43, 226)  — mid-body glow
  - Ice Blue Flare: RGB(135, 206, 250) — bright inner glow
  - Glacial Blue:  RGB(170, 225, 255) — icy accent core
  - Moon White: RGB(230, 235, 255) — cool center point

REFERENCE: SandboxLastPrism/Orbs/SoftGlow.png, SoftGlow64.png,
  feather_circle128PMA.png, circle_05.png
