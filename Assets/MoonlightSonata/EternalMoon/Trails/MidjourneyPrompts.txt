================================================================================
  ETERNAL MOON — TRAIL TEXTURES
  Midjourney Prompt Reference
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Elongated horizontal strip textures mapped onto the sword's trailing arc mesh
  by CalamityStyleTrailRenderer. The trail renderer builds a triangle strip mesh
  that follows the blade tip's world positions over recent frames, then maps
  these textures across the strip UV space. The texture's left edge corresponds
  to the newest (brightest) part of the trail near the blade, and the right edge
  corresponds to the oldest (fading) tail end.

USAGE IN CODE:
  - CalamityStyleTrailRenderer.TrailStyle.Cosmic — primary trail style, uses
    scrolling UVs to animate the trail texture along the strip
  - ScrollingTrailShader.fxc — scrolls texture UV over time for animated flow
  - CelestialValorTrail.fxc — combines trail texture with gradient lookup
  - SimpleTrailShader.fxc — basic trail rendering fallback
  - EternalMoonVFX.WaveTrailColor() modulates the texture with runtime color
  - EternalMoonVFX.WaveTrailWidth() shapes the mesh width (crescent taper)
  - The connecting flow lines in DrawTidalWake() also use trail textures
    stretched between wake points

DIMENSIONS: 256x64 or 512x128 horizontal strips. Left = bright/new,
  Right = fading/old. Can have content extending to all edges since the trail
  mesh handles the shape; the texture provides internal detail and pattern.

COLOR PALETTE:
  - Night Void:     RGB(40, 10, 60)    — trailing fade edge (right side)
  - Deep Indigo:    RGB(75, 0, 130)    — trail body background
  - Violet Body:    RGB(138, 43, 226)  — primary trail energy
  - Ice Blue Flare: RGB(135, 206, 250) — bright trail highlights
  - Glacial Blue:  RGB(170, 225, 255) — accent streaks
  - Moon White: RGB(230, 235, 255) — leading edge cool core (left side)

VISUAL IDENTITY: Flowing water energy, tidal current patterns, moonlight
  ribbons on dark ocean surface. Not fire, not electricity — liquid lunar light.

REFERENCE: SandboxLastPrism/Trails/EnergyTex.png, Trail5Loop.png,
  ThinGlowLine.png, spark_06.png
