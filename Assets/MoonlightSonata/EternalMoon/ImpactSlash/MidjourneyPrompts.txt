================================================================================
  ETERNAL MOON — IMPACT SLASH TEXTURES
  Midjourney Prompt Reference
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Hit impact and slash burst VFX textures that appear at the point of contact
  when the EternalMoon greatsword strikes an enemy. These are instantaneous
  radial burst effects — slash crosses, impact stars, hit sparks, and radial
  shockwave rings that communicate damage feedback to the player. Unlike the
  sweeping slash arcs (which represent the swing itself), impact textures are
  centered on the enemy hit position and burst outward in all directions from
  the point of contact.

USAGE IN CODE:
  - EternalMoonSwing.OnHitNPC() — spawns impact effect at the NPC center or
    the intersection point of the swing arc with the NPC hitbox
  - EternalMoonVFX.DrawImpactBurst() — renders layered impact textures with
    scale-up animation over 4-8 frames (spawn small, expand, fade out)
  - OnHitImpact crit bursts — critical hits spawn enhanced impact with additional
    layers and larger scale using the CritImpactProfile settings
  - CustomParticles.ImpactCross() — 4-directional cross slash particle
  - CustomParticles.HaloRing() — expanding ring shockwave at impact point
  - GodRaySystem.ImpactRadials — radial god-ray lines emanating from hit point
  - All drawn with {A=0} premultiplied alpha and additive blending
  - Impact textures animate by scaling up from 0 to full size over a few frames
    then fading out, so the texture must look good at all intermediate scales

DIMENSIONS: 128x128 or 256x256 square. Centered composition with the impact
  point at the exact center of the canvas. Radial symmetry or near-symmetry
  around the center point. Pure black (#000000) background for additive blending.
  The effect should fill most of the canvas and have smooth falloff to black
  at the edges.

COLOR PALETTE:
  - Night Void:     RGB(40, 10, 60)    — outermost impact halo
  - Deep Indigo:    RGB(75, 0, 130)    — shockwave outer ring, secondary detail
  - Violet:         RGB(138, 43, 226)  — primary impact energy body
  - Lavender:       RGB(180, 150, 255) — mid-tone radial streaks, ring details
  - Ice Blue:       RGB(135, 206, 250) — bright impact flashes, spark tips
  - Moon White:     RGB(240, 235, 255) — central flash point, peak highlights

VISUAL IDENTITY: "Moonrise over dark waters" — impacts should evoke the violence
  of tidal forces, the explosive moment when a wave strikes rock and shatters
  into spray. Lunar cold-light energy released on contact. Crescent and wave
  motifs adapted into radial burst patterns: crescent-shaped spark fragments,
  wave-ripple shockwave rings, tidal foam spray patterns. These are not fire
  explosions — they are cold, luminous, aquatic-lunar energy releases.

REFERENCE: Calamity Mod melee hit effects, Exoblade impact sparks, Ark of the
  Cosmos orbital hit crosses, generic Terraria crit spark textures. Also
  reference water splash photography and tidal impact patterns for organic detail.
