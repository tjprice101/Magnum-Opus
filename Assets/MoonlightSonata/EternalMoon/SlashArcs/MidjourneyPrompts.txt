================================================================================
  ETERNAL MOON — SLASH ARC TEXTURES
  Midjourney Prompt Reference
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Crescent-shaped swing arc VFX textures that appear during EternalMoon melee
  swings. These are the large, dramatic slash arcs that sweep across the screen
  when the player swings the greatsword — not the trailing ribbon behind the
  blade (those are Trails), but the wide slicing energy crescents at the peak
  of each swing. Drawn as additive-blend sprites on black backgrounds, rendered
  at the rotation angle of the swing. The arc represents the concentrated cutting
  energy that extends beyond the physical blade, creating massive sweeping
  crescent shapes inspired by Calamity's Exoblade and Galaxia melee arcs.

USAGE IN CODE:
  - EternalMoonSwing.cs — primary swing projectile, spawns slash arc sprites at
    swing apex with rotation matching the swing angle
  - EternalMoonVFX.DrawSlashArc() — renders the arc texture at the player's
    position with scale, rotation, and color modulation per-frame
  - CrescendoFinaleVFX — enhanced multi-layered slash arcs during the Crescendo
    combo finisher, three overlapping arcs with staggered timing
  - The arc texture is drawn ONCE per swing at peak velocity, not continuously —
    it must read as a single powerful crescent slash, not a smear
  - Additive blending with {A=0} premultiplied alpha for glow stacking
  - Multiple arcs can layer: a dark base arc + bright inner arc + white edge arc

DIMENSIONS: 256x256 or 512x512 square. The arc shape itself is a crescent or
  curved slash within the square canvas, oriented as a roughly diagonal sweep.
  Pure black (#000000) background for additive blending. The arc should occupy
  most of the canvas from corner to corner for maximum visual impact.

COLOR PALETTE:
  - Night Void:     RGB(40, 10, 60)    — outermost arc halo, feathered edge
  - Deep Indigo:    RGB(75, 0, 130)    — arc body outer zone
  - Violet:         RGB(138, 43, 226)  — primary arc energy body
  - Lavender:       RGB(180, 150, 255) — arc body inner glow zone
  - Ice Blue:       RGB(135, 206, 250) — bright arc cutting edge
  - Moon White:     RGB(240, 235, 255) — brightest slash core, edge peak

VISUAL IDENTITY: "Moonrise over dark waters" — these arcs should feel like
  crescent moons slicing through darkness, tidal energy released in a single
  devastating sweep. The arcs carry water-current internal detail, not fire or
  electricity. Think of moonlight reflecting off the curved edge of a massive
  wave crest frozen mid-crash, or the luminous crescent of the moon itself
  weaponized into a cutting arc.

REFERENCE: Calamity Mod — Exoblade slash arcs (massive sweeping crescents),
  Galaxia's layered multi-color arcs, Ark of the Cosmos radial slash effects,
  Celestus orbital disc trails. Also reference FromSoftware Moonlight Greatsword
  projectile arcs from Elden Ring and Dark Souls.
