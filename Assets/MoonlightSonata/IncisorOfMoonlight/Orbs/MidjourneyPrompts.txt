================================================================================
 INCISOR OF MOONLIGHT — ORB TEXTURES
 Midjourney Prompt Reference
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Circular soft-glow textures used as the fundamental building blocks for the
  Incisor's bloom rendering system. The MagnumTextureRegistry provides
  GetSoftGlow() and GetBloom() which return these textures. They are drawn
  additively via the {A=0} premultiplied alpha trick in SpriteBatch.

  Usage in IncisorOfMoonlightVFX:
  - DrawResonantEdgeBloom(): 4-layer bloom at each standing-wave node along
    the blade edge (DeepResonance -> FrequencyPulse -> IceBlue -> HarmonicWhite)
  - DrawConstellationTrail(): Star node glows at recorded blade tip positions
  - Blade-tip bloom: 4-layer glow at the sword tip during swings
  - Phase transition feedback: Expanding glow at player center
  - On-hit impact: Gradient halo rings and bloom bursts

  These orbs are scaled from 0.08x to 2.5x their native resolution and
  tinted to different colors at draw time, so they must be WHITE or NEUTRAL
  at their core with clean feathered edges.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
  - Dimensions: 32x32, 64x64, or 128x128 (square, power-of-two)
  - Background: Pure black (#000000) or transparent alpha
  - Perfectly centered: Brightest pixel at exact center
  - Radially symmetric: Identical brightness in all directions
  - Feathered edges: Brightness falls off smoothly from center to edge
  - Premultiplied alpha ready: Used with {A=0} trick for additive under AlphaBlend
  - Core brightness: Center should be pure white or near-white
  - Edge: Should reach full black/transparent by ~90% radius

COLOR PALETTE (for reference — orbs are drawn white and TINTED at runtime):
  Deep Resonance Purple:  RGB(90, 50, 160)   — #5A32A0
  Frequency Pulse Lavender: RGB(170, 140, 255) — #AA8CFF
  Resonant Silver:        RGB(230, 235, 255)  — #E6EBFF
  Ice Blue Clarity:       RGB(135, 206, 250)  — #87CEFA
  Crystal Edge:           RGB(220, 230, 255)  — #DCE6FF
  Harmonic White:         RGB(235, 240, 255)  — #EBEFFF

NOTE: Unlike flares and trails, orb textures should be MOSTLY WHITE/NEUTRAL
  since they are color-multiplied at draw time. Subtle color tinting in the
  texture itself is acceptable but the core must remain near-white.

VISUAL THEME:
  "Constellation precision — surgical nodes, crystalline cuts, tuning fork
  strikes, star-map geometry."

 POST-PROCESSING NOTES:
  - CRITICAL: These must be perfectly radially symmetric. Use image editor
    tools to verify and correct any asymmetry from generation.
  - The center pixel (or center 2x2 pixels) must be the brightest point.
  - Verify smooth falloff by examining individual rows/columns — there
    should be no visible banding or stepping in the brightness gradient.
  - These will be drawn 4-5 times simultaneously at different scales/colors,
    so any artifacts multiply. Quality must be flawless.
  - The {A=0} premultiplied alpha rendering means black areas become
    fully transparent. Ensure edges reach pure black (#000000).
  - If generating with color, desaturate to pure grayscale in post — the
    mod's runtime tinting handles all coloring.
  - Consider manually creating these in an image editor using radial
    gradient tools for mathematically perfect results. Midjourney outputs
    may need significant cleanup for this category.
  - Test at multiple scales: 0.1x, 0.5x, 1.0x, 2.0x, 3.0x to verify
    the glow reads well across the full range of render sizes.
