================================================================================
 INCISOR OF MOONLIGHT — FLARE TEXTURES
 Midjourney Prompt Reference
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Lens flare and burst-glow textures used for impact moments, charged attack
  flashes, crit hit cascades, and phase-transition feedback. These are drawn
  additively via SpriteBatch (A=0 premultiplied alpha trick) and layered in
  the CustomParticles.GenericFlare() and GodRaySystem.CreateBurst() pipelines.

  The IncisorOfMoonlightVFX system renders 4-5 layered bloom flares on crit
  hits (DeepResonance -> FrequencyPulse -> ResonantSilver -> ConstellationBlue
  -> HarmonicWhite) and a 5-layer mega-cascade on the Phase 3 Crescendo
  finisher. Each flare layer uses a different color and scale.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
  - Dimensions: 128x128 or 256x256 (square, power-of-two)
  - Background: Pure black (#000000) or transparent
  - Format: PNG with alpha channel
  - Centered: Brightest point must be dead-center
  - Additive-ready: Will be rendered with additive blending (black = invisible)
  - Rotationally interesting but readable at any angle

COLOR PALETTE (Incisor of Moonlight — "The Stellar Scalpel"):
  Deep Resonance Purple:  RGB(90, 50, 160)   — #5A32A0
  Frequency Pulse Lavender: RGB(170, 140, 255) — #AA8CFF
  Resonant Silver:        RGB(230, 235, 255)  — #E6EBFF
  Ice Blue Clarity:       RGB(135, 206, 250)  — #87CEFA
  Crystal Edge:           RGB(220, 230, 255)  — #DCE6FF
  Harmonic White:         RGB(235, 240, 255)  — #EBEFFF

VISUAL THEME:
  "Constellation precision — surgical nodes, crystalline cuts, tuning fork
  strikes, star-map geometry."
  Think: astronomical star-point flares, precision instrument optics,
  crystalline refraction bursts, tuning fork resonance shockwaves.
