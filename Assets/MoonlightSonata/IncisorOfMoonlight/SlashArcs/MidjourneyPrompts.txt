The Incisor of Moonlight is NOT a heavy cleaving weapon. Its slashes must
reflect its surgical scalpel identity — thin, precise, laser-sharp arcs. These
are NOT wide sweeping crescents or thick energy waves. Think: laser-scalpel
cuts through reality, constellation connection lines that slash through space,
razor-thin crystalline arc trails that linger momentarily before fading. Every
slash is a calculated incision, not a brute-force sweep.
