================================================================================
  MOONLIGHT'S CALLING — CHANNELING EFFECT TEXTURES
  Asset Category: Arcane Circle / Casting Aura / Gathering Energy Overlays
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  These textures appear around the player and the tome during sustained casting
  to visually communicate that the Moonlight's Calling grimoire is actively
  channeling prismatic moonlight energy. In the mod code, the weapon uses
  ItemUseStyleID.Shoot with autoReuse=true, meaning the player rapidly fires
  MoonlightBeam projectiles in succession. During HoldItem(), the weapon already
  spawns orbiting PrismaticShardDust, LunarMote crescents, StarPointDust
  twinkles, and music note particles around the player. These channeling effect
  textures supplement those particle effects with larger atmospheric overlay
  elements: arcane magic circles that appear at the player's feet or surrounding
  the tome, swirling gathering energy vortices that funnel moonlight into the
  casting point, prismatic light convergence effects where spectral beams gather
  toward the tome from the environment, and musical notation constellations that
  form in the air during sustained channeling. These are rendered as overlay
  sprites drawn with additive blending at the player position, scaled and
  rotated per-frame by the VFX system. They provide the large-scale atmospheric
  "magic is happening here" visual that frames the smaller particle details.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
  - Canvas: 128x128, 256x256, or 512x512 pixels (square format)
  - Background: Pure black (#000000) — rendered with additive blending
  - Center-origin: Primary energy focus should be at or near canvas center
  - Must look good when rotated at any angle (drawn with per-frame rotation)
  - Must look good when scaled between 0.3x and 1.5x of base size
  - Soft feathered edges that fade to black at canvas borders — no hard cutoff
  - Elements should be luminous and translucent, not opaque or solid
  - Should convey sustained magical channeling, not instantaneous detonation
  - Color palette: night void (40,10,60), dark purple (75,0,130),
    violet (138,43,226), prism violet (160,80,255), refraction lavender (180,130,255),
    lavender (180,150,255), refracted blue (100,200,255), ice blue (135,206,250),
    spectral cyan (110,220,255), tome silver (190,200,255),
    moon white (240,235,255)

VISUAL IDENTITY:
  "Prismatic musical refraction — spectral shards, prismatic violet-blue beams,
  musical note motifs, light splitting through crystal. Every beam is moonlight
  through a prism. Playful yet arcane."

CALAMITY STYLE REFERENCES:
  Study the channeling and casting visual effects of Subsuming Vortex and Vivid
  Clarity from Calamity Mod for how sustained magic casting is communicated
  through atmospheric overlays — the sense of arcane power being gathered and
  concentrated before release.
