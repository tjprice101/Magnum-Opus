================================================================================
  MOONLIGHT'S CALLING — TRAIL TEXTURES
  Asset Category: Elongated Trail Strips for Vertex Trail Rendering
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  These are horizontal strip textures mapped onto vertex trail meshes by the
  CalamityStyleTrailRenderer system. In the mod code, PreDraw() builds trail
  vertex arrays from Projectile.oldPos[] (20 cached positions with trailing
  mode 2) and renders them using CalamityStyleTrailRenderer.DrawTrailWithBloom()
  with Cosmic style for primary beams and Ice style for child beams. The trail
  textures are UV-mapped where U spans the trail length (0=head, 1=tail) and V
  spans the trail width (0=top edge, 1=bottom edge). BeamTrailWidth() returns
  12-22 pixels width expanding with bounce count. These textures need visible
  internal detail and energy patterns since they are stretched across the full
  beam length.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
  - Canvas: 256x64 or 512x128 pixels (horizontal strips)
  - Background: Solid black (#000000) — rendered with additive blending
  - Left edge (trail head/origin) should be brightest
  - Right edge (trail tail/tip) should fade toward black or near-black
  - Top and bottom edges must fade to black for smooth trail width blending
  - Internal detail/pattern gives the trail visual interest when stretched
  - Should tile or look seamless when UV-scrolled horizontally by shaders
  - Color palette: dark purple (75,0,130), prism violet (160,80,255),
    violet (138,43,226), refracted blue (100,200,255), ice blue (135,206,250),
    moon white (240,235,255)

VISUAL IDENTITY:
  "Prismatic musical refraction — spectral shards, prismatic violet-blue beams, musical
  note motifs, light splitting through crystal. Every beam is moonlight through
  a prism. Playful yet arcane."
