================================================================================
  MOONLIGHT'S CALLING — FLARE TEXTURES
  Asset Category: Lens Flare / Impact Flash / Bounce Burst Effects
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  These textures are used as lens flare sprites rendered at beam bounce points,
  impact locations, muzzle flashes, and spectral split events. In the mod code,
  they are drawn via CustomParticles.GenericFlare() calls with additive blending
  and {A=0} premultiplied alpha. They appear at every beam-wall bounce (with
  escalating intensity), at NPC hit impacts, during spectral beam splitting on
  bounces 3+, and during the grand finale detonation.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
  - Canvas: 128x128 or 256x256 pixels
  - Background: Pure black (#000000) — will be rendered with additive blending
  - Center-origin: The brightest point must be at exact center of canvas
  - Radial symmetry preferred but not required (asymmetric refraction shapes OK)
  - No hard edges — all elements must fade to black at canvas borders
  - Color palette: dark purple (75,0,130), prism violet (160,80,255),
    violet (138,43,226), refracted blue (100,200,255), ice blue (135,206,250),
    moon white (240,235,255), with subtle spectral purple-blue refraction accents

VISUAL IDENTITY:
  "Prismatic musical refraction — spectral shards, prismatic violet-blue beams, musical
  note motifs, light splitting through crystal. Every beam is moonlight through
  a prism. Playful yet arcane."
