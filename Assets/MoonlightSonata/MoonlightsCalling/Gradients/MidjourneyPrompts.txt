================================================================================
  MOONLIGHT'S CALLING — GRADIENT TEXTURES
  Asset Category: Shader Gradient Strips / Color Lookup Textures
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  These are narrow horizontal gradient strip textures sampled by shaders to
  determine beam color, trail tinting, and particle color cycling. In the mod
  code, BeamTrailColor() interpolates from DarkPurple -> Violet -> PrismViolet
  -> RefractedBlue -> IceBlue -> MoonWhite with shifting based on bounceCount.
  GetRefractionColor() cycles through a spectral hue range starting at purple
  (hue 0.7) expanding with bounces. These gradients are sampled left-to-right
  by UV coordinate, where U=0.0 is the beam origin / trail head and U=1.0 is
  the beam tip / trail tail.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
  - Canvas: 256x16 or 512x32 pixels (wide horizontal strips)
  - Must be seamless-tileable horizontally if intended for looping beam shaders
  - Smooth gradient transitions — no banding artifacts
  - Top and bottom edges should be identical (no vertical variation) unless
    specifically creating a gradient with vertical falloff for beam width
  - Color palette must precisely follow the weapon's spectral progression:
    Left edge: dark purple (75,0,130)
    Early-mid: violet (138,43,226) -> prism violet (160,80,255)
    Center: refracted blue (100,200,255)
    Late-mid: ice blue shimmer (135,206,250)
    Right edge: moon white (240,235,255)
  - Subtle spectral purple-blue undertones may appear between main color stops

VISUAL IDENTITY:
  "Prismatic musical refraction — spectral shards, prismatic violet-blue beams, musical
  note motifs, light splitting through crystal. Every beam is moonlight through
  a prism. Playful yet arcane."
