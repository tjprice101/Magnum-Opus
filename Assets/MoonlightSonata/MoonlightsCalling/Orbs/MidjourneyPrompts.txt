================================================================================
  MOONLIGHT'S CALLING — ORB TEXTURES
  Asset Category: Circular Soft Glow / Bloom Sphere / Halo Textures
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  These are circular soft-glow textures used for bloom rendering, beam body glow,
  and halo effects. In the mod code, DrawBeamBloom() draws a 5-layer bloom stack
  using MagnumTextureRegistry.GetSoftGlow() with {A=0} premultiplied alpha at
  various scales and colors. CustomParticles.HaloRing() also uses circular orb
  textures for the expanding spectral ring effects at bounce points. The orbs
  are also used for the soft circular glow behind music note particles and as
  base textures for the ResonantPulseDust expanding rings.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
  - Canvas: 32x32, 64x64, or 128x128 pixels (various sizes needed)
  - Background: Pure black (#000000) — rendered with additive blending
  - Perfectly centered circular symmetry (or near-circular for organic variants)
  - Soft feathered edges that fade smoothly to black — NO hard circle boundary
  - The brightest region should be a small concentrated center (15-25% of radius)
  - Color palette: dark purple (75,0,130), prism violet (160,80,255),
    violet (138,43,226), refracted blue (100,200,255), ice blue (135,206,250),
    moon white (240,235,255)

VISUAL IDENTITY:
  "Prismatic musical refraction — spectral shards, prismatic violet-blue beams, musical
  note motifs, light splitting through crystal. Every beam is moonlight through
  a prism. Playful yet arcane."
