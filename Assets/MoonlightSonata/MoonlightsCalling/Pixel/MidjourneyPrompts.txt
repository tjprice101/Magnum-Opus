================================================================================
  MOONLIGHT'S CALLING — PIXEL PARTICLE TEXTURES
  Asset Category: Small Pixel-Art Particles / Micro Sprites
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  These are very small sprite textures used for individual particle effects in
  the mod's particle system. PrismaticShardDust uses tiny crystal shard shapes
  that cycle through spectral hues. StarPointDust uses small sharp star/sparkle
  shapes. LunarMote uses crescent shapes. The mod also spawns music note
  particles (MusicNote, QuarterNote, WholeNote, TallMusicNote from the Particles
  folder) along beam trails and at bounce/impact sites. These pixel textures
  supplement the existing particle library with Moonlight's Calling-specific
  prismatic shard fragments and musical notation sprites.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
  - Canvas: 8x8, 16x16, or 32x32 pixels (very small)
  - Background: Pure black (#000000) or transparent — rendered with additive blending
  - Sharp crisp edges preferred (pixel art style, not anti-aliased)
  - Simple recognizable silhouettes at tiny scale
  - Must read clearly at 1:1 pixel scale in a 2D game
  - Bright saturated colors from the palette: prism violet (160,80,255),
    refracted blue (100,200,255), ice blue (135,206,250), moon white (240,235,255)
  - Each prompt generates a SPRITE SHEET of multiple small particle variants
    arranged in a grid on one canvas for efficiency

VISUAL IDENTITY:
  "Prismatic musical refraction — spectral shards, prismatic violet-blue beams, musical
  note motifs, light splitting through crystal. Every beam is moonlight through
  a prism. Playful yet arcane."
