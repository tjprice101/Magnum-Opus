================================================================================
  RESURRECTION OF THE MOON -- TRAIL ASSETS
  Midjourney Prompt Reference
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Elongated horizontal strip textures mapped onto vertex trail meshes via
  the trail shader system. The trail mesh is generated by recording projectile
  positions over time and building a quad strip. The texture is UV-mapped
  so that U runs along the trail length (left=tip, right=tail) and V runs
  across the trail width (0=top edge, 0.5=center, 1=bottom edge).

USAGE IN MOD:
  - Primary comet tail trail behind fired projectile rounds
  - Meteor burn trail left behind fast-moving comet fragments
  - Impact shockwave expanding ring strip (mapped to a circular trail mesh)
  - Ember wake pattern trailing behind the weapon during swing/recoil
  - Resurrection ultimate beam trail

FORMAT REQUIREMENTS:
  - 256x64 or 512x128 PNG (wide horizontal strip)
  - These textures tile horizontally in some cases (noted per prompt)
  - The vertical center line (V=0.5) is the trail core / brightest line
  - Top and bottom edges (V=0, V=1) MUST fade to black for smooth trail borders
  - Black background -- these are rendered with additive blending
  - sRGB color space, 8-bit per channel

COLOR PALETTE:
  Deep Space Violet ........... RGB(50, 20, 100)
  Impact Crater Indigo ........ RGB(100, 80, 200)
  Comet Trail Lavender ........ RGB(180, 120, 255)
  Lunar Shine Blue ............ RGB(120, 190, 255)
  Comet Core Pale Blue ........ RGB(210, 225, 255)
  Blue-White Impact ........... RGB(235, 240, 255)

================================================================================
  POST-GENERATION NOTES:
  - Export all trails at 512x128 for maximum quality (downscale to 256x64
    only if filesize is a concern for the mod build)
  - CRITICAL: Verify top edge row and bottom edge row are pure black (#000000)
    across the entire width; if Midjourney leaves faint glow at borders, paint
    it out manually
  - The Comet Tail (#1) and Resurrection Beam (#5) are NOT horizontally tileable;
    they have distinct left-bright / right-dark directionality
  - The Meteor Burn (#2) and Ember Wake (#4) ARE designed to tile horizontally;
    verify the left and right edges match in color/density for seamless tiling
  - The Shockwave Strip (#3) is used on a circular trail mesh; the left edge
    maps to the leading wavefront and right edge to the dissolving tail
  - In-engine, these textures scroll their U coordinate for animated flow;
    verify that horizontal panning looks good at various scroll speeds
  - Test all trails with the ScrollingTrailShader and SimpleTrailShader to
    ensure correct UV mapping and visual quality
