================================================================================
  RESURRECTION OF THE MOON -- ORB ASSETS
  Midjourney Prompt Reference
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Circular soft-glow textures used for glowing energy orbs, impact shockwave
  rings, radial aura fields, ember cloud volumes, and bullet core sprites.
  These are drawn as additive or alpha-blended sprites and are often scaled,
  rotated, and tinted dynamically in the shader/draw code.

USAGE IN MOD:
  - Comet projectile core glow (drawn behind the projectile sprite)
  - Impact shockwave ring expanding from hit point
  - Residual crater glow lingering after impact
  - Ember cloud volume (multiple orbs overlaid with slight offset + rotation)
  - Resurrection resurrection pulse ring
  - Soft ambient glow behind the weapon when held

FORMAT REQUIREMENTS:
  - 32x32, 64x64, or 128x128 PNG depending on use case
  - Pure black (#000000) background for additive-blend orbs
  - OR transparent background for alpha-blend orbs (noted per prompt)
  - Perfectly centered; the visual center of mass must be at exact center pixel
  - Smooth radial falloff from center to edges -- no hard outlines
  - sRGB color space, 8-bit per channel

COLOR PALETTE:
  Deep Space Violet ........... RGB(50, 20, 100)
  Impact Crater Indigo ........ RGB(100, 80, 200)
  Comet Trail Lavender ........ RGB(180, 120, 255)
  Lunar Shine Blue ............ RGB(120, 190, 255)
  Comet Core Pale Blue ........ RGB(210, 225, 255)
  Blue-White Impact ........... RGB(235, 240, 255)
