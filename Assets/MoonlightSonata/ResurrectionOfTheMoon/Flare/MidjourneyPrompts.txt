================================================================================
  RESURRECTION OF THE MOON -- FLARE ASSETS
  Midjourney Prompt Reference
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Lens flare and burst textures used for hit-impact effects, crit flashes,
  muzzle flash bloom, and comet detonation radiance. These are drawn as
  additive-blended sprites centered on the point of impact or projectile
  origin. They fire for 2-6 frames and fade out rapidly.

USAGE IN MOD:
  - Impact flash when comet rounds strike enemies or terrain
  - Supernova crit burst overlay (scaled up 2-3x with fast fade)
  - Muzzle bloom when the launcher fires
  - Crater detonation secondary flash (delayed 2 frames after primary hit)
  - Resurrection ultimate ability activation burst

FORMAT REQUIREMENTS:
  - 128x128 or 256x256 PNG
  - Pure black (#000000) background (additive blend discards black)
  - Centered radial composition -- glow must originate from dead center
  - No hard rectangular edges; all energy must feather to black at borders
  - sRGB color space, 8-bit per channel, no embedded ICC profile

COLOR PALETTE:
  Deep Space Violet ........... RGB(50, 20, 100)
  Impact Crater Indigo ........ RGB(100, 80, 200)
  Comet Trail Lavender ........ RGB(180, 120, 255)
  Lunar Shine Blue ............ RGB(120, 190, 255)
  Comet Core Pale Blue ........ RGB(210, 225, 255)
  Blue-White Impact ........... RGB(235, 240, 255)
