================================================================================
  RESURRECTION OF THE MOON -- PIXEL PARTICLE ASSETS
  Midjourney Prompt Reference
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Tiny pixel-art scale particle textures for individual sparks, debris shards,
  comet fragment chunks, cinder motes, dust puffs, and micro-embers. These are
  spawned in large quantities by the particle system and each instance is
  individually colored, scaled, rotated, and velocity-driven.

USAGE IN MOD:
  - Ember spark particles flying from comet impact points
  - Rocky debris shards ejected from crater detonations
  - Tiny comet fragment pieces breaking off the main projectile in flight
  - Impact dust motes that linger and float after a hit
  - Burning cinder particles drifting upward from residual crater glow
  - Star-like micro-flare particles for the resurrection ultimate burst

FORMAT REQUIREMENTS:
  - 8x8, 16x16, or 32x32 PNG
  - Sharp pixel-art edges preferred (point-filtered, not bilinear smoothed)
  - Black (#000000) background for additive particles
  - OR transparent background for alpha-blended particles (noted per prompt)
  - These will be point-sampled in-engine; individual pixel clarity matters
  - sRGB color space, 8-bit per channel

COLOR PALETTE:
  Deep Space Violet ........... RGB(50, 20, 100)
  Impact Crater Indigo ........ RGB(100, 80, 200)
  Comet Trail Lavender ........ RGB(180, 120, 255)
  Lunar Shine Blue ............ RGB(120, 190, 255)
  Comet Core Pale Blue ........ RGB(210, 225, 255)
  Blue-White Impact ........... RGB(235, 240, 255)

  POST-GENERATION NOTES:
  - CRITICAL: After Midjourney generation, these MUST be manually cleaned up
    in a pixel art editor (Aseprite, GraphicsGale, or Photoshop with point
    sampling) because Midjourney does not produce true pixel art
  - Manually redraw or clean each sprite to be crisp at the target resolution
  - Use the Midjourney output as a reference/guide for shapes and color placement
  - Final exports should be individual sprite PNGs cut from the sheet, or keep
    as a sheet with known grid dimensions for spritesheet sampling
  - Ember Sparks (#1): cut into individual 8x8 or 12x12 sprites
  - Debris Rocks (#2): cut into individual 16x16 sprites
  - Comet Fragments (#3): cut into individual 14x14 sprites
  - Dust Motes (#4): cut into individual 8x8 sprites (many will be mostly empty)
  - Star Bursts (#5): cut into individual 16x16 sprites
  - Ensure all backgrounds are exactly #000000 for additive blending
  - Test particle spawn rate in-game: embers and dust should be abundant (20-40
    per impact), fragments medium (5-10 per impact), star bursts sparse (3-5
    during ultimate only)
