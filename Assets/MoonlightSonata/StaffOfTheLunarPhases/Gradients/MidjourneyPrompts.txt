STAFF OF THE LUNAR PHASES / GOLIATH OF MOONLIGHT — GRADIENT ASSETS
================================================================================

PURPOSE: Horizontal color gradient strip textures sampled by HLSL shaders for
beam coloring, trail rendering, aura fields, and cosmic energy effects. Shaders
sample these textures using UV coordinates where U maps along the gradient.
Typical sizes: 256x16 or 512x32 pixels. Horizontal orientation, left-to-right
gradient progression.

COLOR PALETTE:
  Cosmic Void:     RGB(20, 8, 40)    — left edge / shadow
  Event Horizon:   RGB(60, 20, 120)  — early shadow
  Gravity Well:    RGB(100, 60, 180) — primary body
  Nebula Purple:   RGB(150, 80, 220) — mid accent
  Energy Tendril:  RGB(180, 140, 255)— bright accent
  Star Core:       RGB(210, 225, 255)— right edge / bright
