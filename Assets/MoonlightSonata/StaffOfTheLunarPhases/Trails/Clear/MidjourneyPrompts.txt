================================================================================
STAFF OF THE LUNAR PHASES / GOLIATH OF MOONLIGHT — CLEAR TRAIL ASSETS
================================================================================

PURPOSE: Same concept as Trails/ but rendered with fully transparent backgrounds
and softer, more diffuse edges. Used for overlay/secondary trail layers that
stack on top of the primary trail for added depth. These are often drawn at
larger scale with lower opacity to create a soft glow halo around the main trail.
Typical size: 256x64 or 512x128 PNG with transparent (alpha=0) background.

KEY DIFFERENCE FROM TRAILS/: These must have transparency (not black background).
The energy/glow shapes float on a fully transparent canvas. Edges must be very
soft and gradual. Used for the outer bloom layer of trail rendering.
