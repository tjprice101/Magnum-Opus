================================================================================
STAFF OF THE LUNAR PHASES / GOLIATH OF MOONLIGHT — TRAIL ASSETS
================================================================================

PURPOSE: Elongated horizontal strip textures (256x64 or 512x128) used for
vertex-based trail rendering via CalamityStyleTrailRenderer and custom trail
shaders. These textures are mapped onto triangle strip meshes that follow
projectile/entity movement. UV.x maps along the trail length, UV.y maps
across the trail width. Used for devastating beam body, small beam trails,
melee swing wakes, and movement trails.

VISUAL IDENTITY: Gravitational wake — cosmic energy streams, accretion flow
patterns, nebula ribbon textures, warped spacetime trails.
