================================================================================
  GOLIATH OF MOONLIGHT — BEAM TEXTURE COLLECTION
  Midjourney Prompt Collection
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Generate elongated horizontal strip textures for beam rendering used by
  the Goliath of Moonlight, the massive cosmic minion summoned by the Staff
  of the Lunar Phases. The Goliath fires devastating beams, gravitational
  rays, and concentrated stellar energy blasts. These textures will be
  mapped onto beam trail primitives and stretched/tiled along the beam's
  length in the game engine. They must tile horizontally and have clean
  vertical fade-to-black edges for additive blending.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
  - Elongated horizontal strip format (wide aspect ratio, ~8:1 or wider)
  - Pure black background RGB(0, 0, 0) for additive blending compositing
  - Beam energy concentrated in the vertical center, fading to black at
    top and bottom edges (vertical falloff gradient)
  - Horizontally tileable or seamlessly repeatable patterns preferred
  - No hard edges — all luminous elements must gradient-fade to black
  - Suitable for additive, screen, or lighten blend modes in rendering
  - Color values must stay within the Moonlight Sonata palette

MOONLIGHT SONATA COLOR PALETTE:
  Night Void:    RGB(40, 10, 60)   — deepest shadow, beam outer edges
  Deep Indigo:   RGB(75, 0, 130)   — darkness, beam body shadows
  Violet:        RGB(138, 43, 226) — primary energy, beam core glow
  Lavender:      RGB(180, 150, 255) — mid-tone, beam body fill
  Ice Blue:      RGB(135, 206, 250) — bright accent, energy highlights
  Moon White:    RGB(240, 235, 255) — brightest core, peak intensity

BEAM IDENTITY:
  The Goliath of Moonlight is a gravity-warping cosmic colossus. Its beams
  are not simple laser blasts — they are focused gravitational distortion
  rays that warp light and space along their path. Beams should exhibit
  gravitational lensing at their edges, accretion-disk-like energy flow,
  stellar compression at the core, and nebula-like turbulence in their
  mid-layers. The beams are the Goliath's voice in the cosmic symphony
  that the summoner conducts.
