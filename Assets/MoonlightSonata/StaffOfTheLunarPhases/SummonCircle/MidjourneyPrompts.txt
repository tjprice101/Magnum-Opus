================================================================================
  STAFF OF THE LUNAR PHASES — SUMMONING CIRCLE TEXTURES
  Midjourney Prompt Collection
================================================================================

PURPOSE:
  Generate summoning circle and ritual effect textures that appear when
  the player uses the Staff of the Lunar Phases to summon the Goliath of
  Moonlight. These are top-down circular/radial effect textures rendered
  on black backgrounds for additive blending. They represent cosmic
  summoning circles, gravitational well portals, phase-transition rings,
  orrery rotation patterns, and nebula convergence phenomena. The visual
  narrative: the summoner raises the baton, the summoning circle opens
  as a gravitational portal, and the Goliath of Moonlight emerges from
  the cosmic rift. Each texture should feel like a window into deep space
  being forced open by sheer gravitational will.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
  - Square or near-square format (1:1 aspect ratio)
  - Pure black background RGB(0, 0, 0) for additive blending compositing
  - Radially symmetric or near-symmetric design centered in the frame
  - All luminous elements must gradient-fade to black at the outer edges
  - No hard outer boundaries — soft radial falloff to pure black
  - Suitable for additive, screen, or lighten blend modes
  - Should rotate smoothly when animated (no directional bias that would
    look wrong when spinning)
  - Color values strictly within the Moonlight Sonata palette

MOONLIGHT SONATA COLOR PALETTE:
  Night Void:    RGB(40, 10, 60)   — deepest shadow, void center
  Deep Indigo:   RGB(75, 0, 130)   — darkness, outer ring elements
  Violet:        RGB(138, 43, 226) — primary energy, active circle lines
  Lavender:      RGB(180, 150, 255) — mid-tone, glyph fills, ambient glow
  Ice Blue:      RGB(135, 206, 250) — bright accent, stellar highlights
  Moon White:    RGB(240, 235, 255) — brightest highlight, peak luminance

SUMMONING IDENTITY:
  The summoning ritual is a gravitational invocation — the staff opens a
  localized spacetime distortion that serves as a gateway from which the
  Goliath materializes. The circle is part arcane ritual diagram, part
  gravitational well cross-section, part orrery schematic, part orchestral
  score rendered in cosmic geometry. The summoner is a conductor beginning
  a performance; the summoning circle is the first note struck.
