# Worm/Serpent Boss Designs - Enigma Variations & Fate

## Overview

These prompts generate Terraria-style worm boss sprites with **TOP-VIEW** perspective for segment linking. Each boss has:
- **Head** - Front-facing, menacing, leads the worm
- **Body Segment** - Repeatable middle section, links together
- **Tail** - Terminal piece, trailing effects

All designs feature **PRISTINE METALLIC CONSTRUCTION** with musical theming and vibrant colors matching their respective themes.

The Enigma worm boss represents **THE UNANSWERED QUESTION** - an insanely unique celestial serpent made of contrasting white and black metal with silver ornate royal mechanism accents. Music notes physically surround and orbit the creature. The design radiates a powerful mysterious aura, ignited in both purple AND green arcane flames dancing together. Mysteries float around it including watching eyes and floating stars. Every segment should feel like an epic elegant design created by music itself.

# IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

## Segment Connection Guidelines

When implementing in Terraria:
1. **Head** connects at its BOTTOM edge to Body segments
2. **Body segments** connect at BOTH top and bottom to other body segments or head/tail
3. **Tail** connects at its TOP edge to the final body segment

## VFX Recommendations

### Enigma Wyrm (Celestial Mystery VFX)
- Dual purple AND green flame particle trails dancing together
- Music notes physically orbiting the entire worm
- Watching eyes occasionally blink and track independently
- Floating stars drifting around the creature
- Question marks spawning at segment joints
- Arcane glyphs orbit the entire worm in rings
- Reality shimmer distortion around edges
- Silver sparkle particles on ornate accents
- Powerful mysterious aura glow radiating outward

### Fate Serpent (MAXIMUM VFX)
- Constant chromatic aberration shader on sprite
- Temporal echo afterimages (multiple trailing sprites)
- Reality crack particles spawning at head
- Prismatic particle trail white → pink → crimson → void
- Screen shake on segment reveal
- Dimension tear VFX at tail tip
- Musical notes exploding on attacks
- The entire boss should feel like it's BARELY contained by reality

## Prompt Modifiers Reference

| Modifier | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| `--s 750` | Standard stylization for consistent results |
| `--s 900` | Higher stylization for Fate segments |
| `--s 1000` | Maximum stylization for ultimate head/tail |
| `--v 7.0` | Midjourney v7 for best metallic rendering |
| `--ar 1:1` | Square for head sprites |
| `--ar 2:3` | Portrait for body segments |
| `--ar 1:2` | Tall portrait for tail |
| `--style raw` | Raw style for maximum detail on ultimate pieces |
| `--quality 2` | Double quality for final renders |
