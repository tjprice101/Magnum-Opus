# ENIGMA VARIATIONS UNIQUE WEAPON PROMPTS

## Design Philosophy

Enigma Variations (Elgar) features a celestial-mystery aesthetic born from music itself:
- **Base Materials**: White celestial metal and black void metal in elegant contrast
- **Accent Material**: Silver ornate trim like royal clockwork mechanisms
- **Energy Effects**: Dual purple and green arcane flames dancing together, radiating powerful aura
- **Mystery Elements**: Watching eyes embedded throughout, floating mysteries and stars, question mark motifs, arcane glyphs
- **Musical Elements**: Music notes physically surrounding and orbiting the weapon, treble clefs, staff lines woven into the design
- **Atmosphere**: Insanely unique, epic and elegant, celestial mystery created by music itself
- **Design Style**: Royal mechanism aesthetic, ornate silver detailing, full weapon visibility

These prompts feature insanely unique celestial weapons that embody mystery and music, surrounded by watching eyes, floating stars, and dual-colored arcane flames.
