## 1. Pre-Hardmode Foundation

*Work Bench crafts. The very first accessories available.*

#### Composer's Notebook 
- **Effects:**
  - +5% Melee and Ranger Damage
  - +5% Attack Speed 
- **Lore:** *"The first steps of a grand composition"*
- **Recipe:** 5ﾃ・Resonant Crystal Shard + 1ﾃ・Faded Sheet Music + 1ﾃ・Book @ Work Bench

#### Resonant Pendant 
- **Effects:**
  - +3% Magic and Summoner Damage
  - +8% Attack and Summon Speed
- **Lore:** *"Attuned to the frequencies of creation"*
- **Recipe:** 1ﾃ・Tuning Fork + 1ﾃ・Dull Resonator + 3ﾃ・Resonant Crystal Shard @ Work Bench
> **Used in:** Melodic Charm

#### Melodic Charm 
- **Effects:**
  - +10% All Damage
  - +15 Mana Regeneration/second
  - +12% Attack and Summon Speed
- **Lore:** *"The melody begins to take shape"*
- **Recipe:** 1ﾃ・Composer's Notebook + 1ﾃ・Resonant Pendant + 5ﾃ・Minor Music Note @ Work Bench
> **Used in:** Adagio Pendant (Moonlight), Badge of Valor (Eroica), Chime of Flames (La Campanella)
