                       MUSICAL TERMINOLOGY REFERENCE
================================================================================

Cadence      - Chord progression ending a phrase
Fugue        - Complex interwoven melodies
Tacet        - Instruction to remain silent
Fermata      - Hold a note indefinitely
Fortissimo   - Very loud (ff)
Sforzando    - Sudden emphasis (sfz)
Leitmotif    - Recurring theme associated with a person/idea
Coda         - Concluding passage of a piece
Cadenza      - Solo virtuosic passage
Opus         - A musical work (numbered compositions)
Nocturne     - Night music, typically piano
Refrain      - Repeating section of a song
Glissando    - Sliding between notes
Diminished   - Chord type with unsettling, tense sound
Suspended    - Chord that creates tension awaiting resolution
Atonal       - Music without a key center (dissonant)
Crescendo    - Gradually getting louder
Reprise      - Return of a musical theme
