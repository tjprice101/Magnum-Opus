================================================================================
                              OVERVIEW
================================================================================

MagnumOpus features 10 post-Moon Lord themes in a LINEAR PROGRESSION,
plus 4 seasonal bosses woven into pre-Moon Lord hardmode.

Each post-Moon Lord theme boss drops a Treasure Bag containing 2-3 random
weapons from that theme's pool + crafting materials.

Exception: Moonlight Sonata (Tier 1) weapons are CRAFTED at the Moonlight
Anvil because vanilla Moon Lord serves as the gate (no custom boss).

CRAFTING STATIONS (2 Tiers):
  Tier 1 — Moonlight Furnace / Moonlight Anvil (Tiers 1-6)
  Tier 2 — Fate's Cosmic Furnace / Fate's Cosmic Anvil (Tiers 7-10)

Fate (Tier 6) is a MAJOR MILESTONE — like Wall of Flesh for hardmode.
Defeating Fate (must be defeated TWICE — True Form) unlocks Tier 2 stations.

Clair de Lune (Tier 10) is the FINAL tier. No Mercury or Mars content.

================================================================================
                    MATERIAL FLOW SUMMARY
================================================================================

Each post-Moon Lord theme has:
  • Resonance Ore — spawns in world after previous boss defeated
  • Remnant — drops from mining the ore
  • Resonant Core — smelted from remnants at furnace
  • Harmonic Core — crafted from cores at anvil
  • Resonant Energy — boss treasure bag drop
  • Theme Shards — dropped by theme-specific enemies
  • Theme Essence — 2% from theme enemies

MOONLIGHT SONATA WEAPONS use: Energy + Cores + Shards (crafted)
ALL OTHER WEAPONS come from: Boss Treasure Bags (2-3 per bag)
ACCESSORIES are: Crafted at Moonlight Anvil (T1-6) or Fate's Cosmic Anvil (T7-10)

================================================================================
                    ACCESSORY PROGRESSION
================================================================================

6 PROGRESSIVE CHAINS (one per playstyle):
  Defense, Melee, Ranger, Mage, Mobility, Summoner
  Each chain has items for every tier, crafted at the appropriate anvil.

THEME-SPECIFIC ACCESSORIES:
  Each theme has 2-6 unique accessories + wings (where applicable).
  Crafted at the tier-appropriate anvil.

COMBINATION ACCESSORIES:
  Two-theme combos (6), Three-theme combos (4), Grand combos (5),
  Ultimate: Coda of Absolute Harmony

SEASONAL ACCESSORIES (16):
  3 per season + 4 combination items
  Crafted at Mythril Anvil
