================================================================================
                    MAGNUM OPUS - MOD PROGRESSION
================================================================================

This document outlines the complete progression system for the Harmony of 
Synchronicity mod, including implemented features and planned content.

================================================================================
                              OVERVIEW
================================================================================

The mod features 13 classical music themes in a LINEAR PROGRESSION.
Each boss must be defeated to unlock the next theme's content.

Each theme includes:
  • Resonant Ores (spawned after previous boss kill)
  • Harmonic Essence (dropped by theme's boss - REQUIRED for crafting)
  • Resonant Weapons & Tools
  • Crafting Station Upgrades (at Fate and Clair de Lune milestones)

CRAFTING RULE: You CANNOT craft any theme's equipment without that theme's
               Harmonic Essence, which only drops from that theme's boss.
