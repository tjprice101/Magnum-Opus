Magnum Opus - A musical post-Moon Lord content mod!

After defeating the Moon Lord, a mystical Grand Piano appears in the world, and Resonance Ores begin to generate throughout the underground.

Features:
- Moonlight Sonata content: New ore, crafting stations, tools, and weapons themed after Beethoven's Moonlight Sonata
- Eroica content: New ore, materials, and a challenging boss fight - Eroica, God of Valor
- Custom rarities with animated colors
- Unique boss mechanics with three movement minions

Craft the Score of Eroica and use it at the Grand Piano to summon the boss!
