[h1]Magnum Opus[/h1]
A musical post-Moon Lord content mod that adds Beethoven-themed items, ores, and bosses!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Moonlight Sonata content[/b]: New ore, crafting stations (Moonlight Furnace & Anvil), powerful tools, and unique weapons
[*] [b]Eroica content[/b]: New ore, crafting materials, and a challenging boss fight - Eroica, God of Valor
[*] [b]Grand Piano[/b]: A mystical structure that spawns after Moon Lord's defeat - used to summon bosses
[*] [b]Custom rarities[/b]: Animated color effects for rare items
[*] [b]Unique boss mechanics[/b]: The Eroica boss features three movement minions representing symphony movements
[/list]

[h2]Getting Started[/h2]
After defeating the Moon Lord:
1. A Grand Piano will spawn somewhere in your world
2. Resonance Ores will generate underground
3. Mine the ores and craft new equipment
4. Craft the Score of Eroica to summon the boss!
